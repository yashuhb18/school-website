Who We Are

A Legacy of Learning & Leadership

Vagbharathi Vidhya Samasthe is a premier high school nestled in the serene surroundings of Hosahundi, Mysore. For over two decades, we have been committed to providing holistic education that goes beyond textbooks — building character, curiosity, and courage in every student.

Our philosophy blends the richness of Indian tradition with the demands of a modern, global world. We believe every child carries a unique spark, and our role is to fan that flame into a lifelong pursuit of excellence.
